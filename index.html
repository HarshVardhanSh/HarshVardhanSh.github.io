About

I am an M.Sc. student in Machine Learning at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence, advised by Prof. Kun Zhang, Prof. Junpei Komiyama, and Prof. Jin Tian.

Current focus

Causal machine learning for decisions

My current research studies causal inference and causal decision-making, including fixed-confidence best-arm identification for causal mediation analysis, precision-oriented evaluation for individual causal effect prediction, and causal reinforcement learning.

Background

Research and industry ML experience
