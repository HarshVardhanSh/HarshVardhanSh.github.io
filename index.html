Fixed-Confidence Best-Arm Identification for Causal Mediation Analysis

Harsh Shrivastava, Yuta Kawakami, Junpei Komiyama, Jin Tian.

We formulate natural direct effect maximization as a fixed-confidence best-arm identification problem, derive instance-dependent sample-complexity lower bounds, and develop an asymptotically optimal Track-and-Stop-style algorithm.

Measuring Precision in Individual Causal Effect Prediction

Yuta Kawakami*, Harsh Shrivastava*, Jin Tian.

We analyze the evaluation of individual causal effect prediction under latent heterogeneity, identify limitations of PEHE, and develop precision-oriented error metrics.
